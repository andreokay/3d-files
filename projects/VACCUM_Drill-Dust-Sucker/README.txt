# Summary

Wanna hang a picture? But don't like the dust tickeling down on your sofas?
This adapter for your vacuum will stick to the wall by the mighty vacuum force and suck away all the dust where it is produced. As long as it is an Elektrolux vacuum cleaner, that is. 
Otherwise you might want to model an adapter for your vacuum cleaner or use some of your blue tape stock as a makestift adapter.
